Define Roles & Responsibilities

When hiring an offshore eCommerce app development company for your business, defining each team member’s roles & responsibilities is essential. Doing this will help ensure they complete the project on time and to the highest standards. 

Also, it helps in stating clearly what each offshore team member is responsible for and makes them accountable for achieving them. This can help avoid any confusion or misunderstandings. 

Following are some typical roles & responsibilities that need to be assigned: 

  • Project Manager:  A project manager manages your eCommerce development project. They oversee the budget, timeline, and deliverables and ensure the progress of your project as planned.  
  • Developers:  A developer is the backbone of an eCommerce development project which not only writes the code but also implements the technical aspects of the project. They work closely with the technical lead to ensure the code is high quality and meets the project requirements.

  • Quality Assurance (QA) Analyst: QA analysts are responsible for ensuring the quality of the eCommerce website. They test the website for bugs, errors, and usability issues and work closely with the developers to resolve any problems. There are many types of QA analysts, so it depends on your project requirements.  
  • Technical Lead: Technical team is incomplete without its leader, so you would also need a technical lead for your project. A technical lead handles the technical aspects of the project. Establish Effective Communication

Maintaining good communication with an offshore team is crucial for successfully managing a software development project. Regular meetings with the development team, setting clear expectations and guidelines, and establishing suitable communication protocols help ensure efficiency and quality of work.

By having consistent and open communication, businesses can track progress and promptly address any issues. Here are some of the best ways you can establish effective communication with your offshore team:

Monitor Progress & Performance

As you’ll move forward with the planning and implementation, make sure you monitor your offshore team. They might operate from a different country, but you must have control over the software development process. 

Tracking the progress & performance of your eCommerce app development company is vital to be updated with the project development and challenges. Here are some tips on how to monitor & manage an offshore software development team:

  • Establish a Project Schedule: A project schedule outlines each task’s timeline and will help you set the deadline for each milestone. This will lead to a smooth monitoring of progress and identify any potential delays early on.
  • Set Performance Metrics & KPIs: Establish key performance indicators (KPIs) and metrics that can support you in measuring project performance. You can use metrics like time spent on tasks, budget spent, and quality of work to track progress and identify areas for improvement.
  • Address Issues Promptly: If any issues and concerns arise, address them as a priority. Be proactive in resolving any problems affecting the project’s progress or quality. Encourage team members to share their concerns and provide feedback.
  • Analyze & Monitor Project Risks: Identify potential risks impacting the project’s success and develop a risk management plan to mitigate them. Regularly review the plan and make necessary adjustments.
  • Celebrate Milestones: Celebrate project milestones and recognize the team’s achievements. If you boost the morale and motivation of the tea, they will be able to achieve project objectives sooner.

By following these tips, you can ensure that you are monitoring the progress and performance of your eCommerce app development company effectively. This will help you get the most out of your offshore development team and ensure the project succeeds.
