What is WooCommerce? 

As known, it’s a WordPress plugin, which adds e-commerce functionality to your WordPress website for efficiently running a web store. With just a few clicks, you can manipulate a WordPress website into a fully-functional e-commerce website. 

Among entrepreneurs, who want to thrive in business, WooCommerce experts would give impetus to the e-commerce platform.

What does WooCommerce do?

WooCommerce entails basic store functionality right out of the box, with a free plugin available from the plugin repository or the WordPress dashboard.

It includes available loads of extensions – more thereon during a bit! – and offering incredible functions right out of the box.

Why WooCommerce?

WooCommerce is a popular open-source, utterly customizable e-commerce platform for entrepreneurs across the globe. Hence, giving your e-Commerce business new horizons beyond the confines of traditional e-commerce solutions, penetrating your imagination.

Given Below Are The Ten Advantages of WooCommerce :

1) Easy Installation, Setup, and Integration

WooCommerce is meant to kick-start things as soon as possible, with an easier installation and setup wizard to urge your e-commerce store up and running together with your WordPress theme.

WooCommerce Setup Wizard

  • Installation –The WooCommerce plugin is installed similar to other plugins; look for it within the Plugins options in your WordPress dashboard, and install and activate with a few clicks.
  • Setup – Once you install WooCommerce, the Setup Wizard will walk you through configuring steps to urge your store up and running. The e-commerce options are virtually endless, and therefore the setup wizard cuts to the chase and highlights vital opportunities to professionals, so you never miss a step.

2) WordPress Theme Integration 

 Since WooCommerce is compatible with WordPress, it works well with most WordPress themes. It allows selecting the Storefront theme if you’d wish to use the official music– a bit like the plugin, and freed from charge!

3) Data Control- Giving Assurance With High-Stake Projects

It doesn’t cause you to worry about losing control of your data – WooCommerce will put you in the driver’s seat.

4) Data Protection – Don’t Allow Invader to Misuse Data

When you use a third-party platform for e-commerce, if they close, your data is lost. With WooCommerce, you can hire WooCommerce Programmers to get complete control over data. Data associated with customer purchase history to store performance critical for running a successful e-commerce website.

5) Feature-packed Shopping Cart- Gives Vital Options

Tailor your site visitor’s checkout process from start to end with strategies that help convert visitors into customers. From payment gateway to see out URLs, or find out your site your way.

6) Payment Gateways – Ensures No Hassles In Making Payments

With WooCommerce, you can accept checks, cash on delivery, bank transfer, or use PayPal to accept major credit cards and other PayPal payments. Prefer Stripe? You’ll configure stripe as a payment gateway using the Setup Wizard.

7) Geo-Location and Automatic Taxes – No Hidden Charges/ Cost

WooCommerce gives you a more straightforward choice to activate geolocation and automatic taxes; your shop will display the right price, including taxes, to your customers, regardless of the location.

8) Customizable Checkout Process – Ensures Customer Convenience

You are controlling the checkout process from start to end. You’ll prefer to sell to specific countries or worldwide, set your checkout flow URLs, enable/disable guest checkout, and force secure checkout.

9) Default Currency – No Discrepancy In Currency

An in-depth range of obtainable currencies help choose the currency you would like to use for the shop.

10) Redirect Cart- Adds Flexibility To The Cart

They give you the most straightforward choice to redirect customers to their cart once the product is added.
