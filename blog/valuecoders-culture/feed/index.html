What Is ISO 9001:2015

ISO’s Quality Management Systems-Requirements (QMS) series of standards is a suite of certifications that cover vital topics such as quality management, supplier management, product realization, and service provision. ISO 9001:2015 provides businesses with requirements for designing, implementing, and maintaining a quality management system to ensure customer satisfaction by meeting customer needs regarding features and time requirements.

The specification requires processes to be continually evaluated, enhanced, or revised to meet the company’s changing business needs. In addition, it can help the company build and maintain a culture of continual improvement, leading to sustainable performance improvements for all products or services offered. The standard also includes provisions on records management, corrective action procedures, and configuration control.

About Capability Maturity Model Integration (CMMI)

Capability Maturity Model Integration, or CMMI, is a framework for improving software development organizations’ processes and product quality. The Carnegie Mellon Software Engineering Institute developed CMMI. The model’s five maturity levels increase organizational capability and rigor. Here are the five maturity levels:

1. Initial — New businesses go through an unpredictable environment that leads to increased risks and inefficiency at this stage.

2. Managed– At this level, companies have formalized their software engineering practices with documented policies & procedures.

3. Defined — These companies have documented plans for improvement at this stage.

4. Quantitatively Managed — Companies have measurement systems that quantify how well they’re meeting performance targets in the project management’s six dimensions (quality attributes) in process areas.

5. Optimizing — Companies must establish metrics and quantitative goals for each maturity level before progressing to the next one. You may outsource some aspects of your software development process at this stage if you don’t have the staff available internally.

The final stage occurs when an organization has achieved all other stages, has established its own metrics system from within, and can reliably assess its progress in improving productivity, efficiency and effectiveness over time through iterative activities.

Benefits of CMMI in Software Development

Capability Maturity Model Integration (CMMI) is a complete process improvement model for developing, maintaining, and acquiring software systems. It is built upon eight capability areas:

  • Planning, organizing, staffing, directing & controlling the work
  • Analysis & design
  • Implementation and testing
  • Operation & maintenance
  • Evaluation of results
  • Problem detection
  • Diagnosis
  • Correction

CMMI provides a structured set of processes that can be tailored to meet the specific needs of any organization. CMMI is an invaluable asset to any organization that seeks to reduce costs, improve efficiency and enhance performance.

If the new member has joined the team, then the management team should accurately provide them guidance related to check-in and check out.

Top 10+ Tools For Effective Remote Team Management

Numerous tools can help your organization in managing the remote team, but all the tools do not offer the foremost result. Here I am listing a few tools that proffer leading solutions to manage the remote team.

  • Google Hangouts: This tool is free and allows you to make group video calls. In one group video call, 17 people can take part, and it allows users to share screens.

  • I Done This: It is an online tool that allows the team to present daily updates by replying to an email at the end of each workday. It also includes a work activities list.
